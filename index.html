About Shree Chamunda Trust

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to bridging the gap between communities and essential services through innovative technology solutions.

Our Mission

To empower underserved communities by providing accessible information, resources, and support through technology-driven solutions.

Our Values

Compassion, Innovation, Inclusivity, Transparency, and Community-Driven Impact guide everything we do.

Who We Serve

We support marginalized communities, including low-income families, refugees, and individuals facing barriers to essential services.
